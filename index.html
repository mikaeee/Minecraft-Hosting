What we do

We currently only sell Minecraft servers, but will support over 50 other game servers in the future. My bud and I were always looking for the perfect, affordable Minecraft server until we came up with the idea of hosting one ourselves. We want to give you the best experience possible, with a 24/7 support and a totally free experience for a limited time. All we want from you is your feedback.
